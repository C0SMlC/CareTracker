How it works

Benefit your health in 3 easy steps

01

Download and Install CareTracker

CareTracker is available for free on both iOS and Android devices. Once downloaded, simply follow the prompts to install the app. After installation, you'll need to create an account or sign in with an existing account.

02

Connect Your Fitness Tracker

CareTracker connects with a variety of fitness trackers. To connect your fitness tracker, Follow the prompts to connect your device and grant the necessary permissions.

03

Start Earning Money

Once your device is connected, CareTracker will begin tracking your steps automatically. Join challenges of your choice, compete with other players, and earn a reward.

Features

Your health is our top priority.

After all health is what matters

Caretracker uses your smartphone or connected fitness tracker to automatically track your steps throughout the day. This feature allows you to easily monitor your daily activity level and see how close you are to meeting your fitness goals.

Join challenges of your choice, compete with other players

Caretracker provides a platform for health enthusiasts to compete with one other, the challenge based system is what makes it so special, beat other players and earn rewards.

Yeah, you read it right, you get reward to keep yourself healthy.

CareTracker offers a rewards program where you can earn cash rewards for every step you take. The more you walk, the more you earn, which can be redeemed for cash or gift cards. This feature helps incentivize healthy habits and encourages you to stay active.
